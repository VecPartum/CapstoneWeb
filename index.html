Coexistence

The Story

Coexistence is an action story-driven RPG set in a world where humanity has been punished for its destruction of the natural world. To seek redemption, and restore the world, you must embark on a journey to heal the land and reconnect with the spirits of nature.

Inspired by classic action-adventures like The Legend of Zelda and Ōkami, we set out to craft a world that feels alive, magical, and worth saving. Every region you restore blooms back to color. Every spirit you free has a story to tell.

About A4th

Who We Are

A4th is a team of 4 students making our original game as our capstone project. We were inspired by classic "retro" action-adventure games.

Coexistence is the culmination of our shared vision to create our first game aligned with United Nations' SDG 15 (Life on Land).

We're learning as we go, and we're proud of what we're making together.
